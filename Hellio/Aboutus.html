About Transtrade

Building smarter delivery solutions with HailO & Delis to make everyday life easier.

Who We Are

Transtrade is a technology-driven logistics company focused on transforming how cities handle deliveries. With our platforms HailO and Delis, we connect customers, businesses, and delivery partners in one seamless ecosystem.

Our goal is to simplify everyday needs — whether it’s ordering food, delivering medicines, or sending parcels across the city.

🚀 Our Mission

To provide fast, reliable, and secure delivery services through smart technology.

🌍 Our Vision

To become a leading urban logistics platform connecting every corner of the city.

Our Platforms

HailO

An all-in-one delivery app for food, groceries, medicines and parcel services.

Delis

A smart logistics system connecting users, shops and delivery partners efficiently.
